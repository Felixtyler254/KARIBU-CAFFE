What is Karibu?

Karibu is a kenyan inspired cafe based in Nairobi,Kiambu and Kericho. Karibu is a Kenyan custom where people welcome each other to relax, eat cake, have drinks and spend time, usually in a relaxing environment.

KARIBU CAFFE has the same concept. Come and meet the KARIBU CAFFE team and enjoy some of the delicious snacks and drinks on offer.
